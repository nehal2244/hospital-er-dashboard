⚠️ Risk Areas
Late-night hours (11PM–1AM) see highest volume — overnight staffing is understaffed
Children (0–17) form 21% of ER — need pediatric fast-track lane
Cardiology & Neurology have highest critical severity rates — resource intensive
73% of satisfaction data is missing — survey participation must improve
Elderly (70+) form 12.5% — need priority fast-track protocols
General Practice overloaded at 1,840 visits — needs overflow clinic
✅ Recommendations
👨‍⚕️Staffing: Increase nursing staff by 20% on Saturdays & Sunday nights. Add 2 triage nurses during 10PM–2AM shift.
🚀Fast Track: Open a fast-track lane for Low severity patients to cut wait by est. 8–10 min.
🔄Referral Process: Strengthen primary care referral pathways — 58% walk-ins strain capacity.
🧠Neurology: Add 1 on-call neurologist during peak hours. Avg wait 36.8 min is highest among all departments.
📱Satisfaction: Deploy tablet-based surveys at discharge to capture more feedback in real time.
